About Mockify

Created for designers, by designers

Mockify was born from a simple need: creating realistic chat and social media mockups without the hassle of complex design tools. As UI/UX designers ourselves, we found the existing solutions either too complicated or not realistic enough.

Our mission is to provide designers, developers, content creators, and educators with a tool that's both powerful and easy to use. Whether you're prototyping a new app, creating social media content, or just having fun, Mockify has you covered.

All data generated stays in your browser. We believe in privacy by design, which means we don't store your conversations, personal information, or generated content on our servers.
